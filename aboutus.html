About Us

Rattle was founded by Dr. Will Afton early in the year of 1987 as he discovered that trough vibrations sent at the molicular scale to rodents, a process similar to mitosis can be initiated leading to a cloning of the rat. With this knowledge he invented the first prototype of the rat duplicator, which would later be upgraded into the version our company uses today.
